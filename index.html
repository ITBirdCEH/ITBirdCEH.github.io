Who can you contact? 

If you are under 19 and need to talk to someone in confidence you can call ChildLine on 0800 11 11or you can also visit the ChildLine website to chat to a counsellor online.  

User Names   

Children should choose a screen name that is memorable, but that doesn't allow strangers to identify them in real life.    

Just Talk  

Other people that you could talk to is your parents or a trusted adult such as a teacher. These can be the best people to talk to and they will help you keep safe online.   

Do you know who you are talking to or playing games with?        

Don't play with people you don't know! This is a common mistake that people make all the time. Trusting players on videogames, even though you have never met can lead to danger. Never give away your home address or personal details online.        

Watch out for cyber bullying   

This is a problem that many people deal with in their lives and you need to remember YOU DONT NEED TO TALK BACK. If you feel uncomftable in a conversation, just leave it and tell someone you trust. You don't have to stay if you are not enjoying it.     

Have a lot of fun!!! 

The best things about the internet is the fun that you can have on it; reading facts or playing games with your friends. The biggest rule of all is to enjoy it and stay safe.
